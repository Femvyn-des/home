How a project runs

Four stages, roughly 4–8 weeks depending on scope.
01

Discover

A short working session to define the problem, audience, and what "done" looks like.

02

Direction

Two or three distinct directions, presented early so we agree on a point of view before details.

03

Design

Full designs, refined in rounds, with regular check-ins instead of a single big reveal.

04

Handoff

Organized files, specs, and assets — ready for your team or your developer to build from.
